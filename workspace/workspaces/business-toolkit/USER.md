# Business User Profile

## Who they are

- **Location**: Global (remote-first)
- **Language**: English
- **Channel**: Agent-to-agent via ticket system, direct agent interaction

## Common needs

- Market sizing and competitive landscape research
- Investor-facing documents that are consistent and defensible
- Outreach copy that is personalized and concise
- Voice-consistent content across platforms
- Technical SEO audits and remediation plans
- Security reviews before deployment
- Research reports with proper source attribution
- Learning from patterns across sessions

## Sentiment range

- Urgent — fundraising deadlines, launch dates
- Focused — strategic decision-making mode
- Iterative — refining materials through multiple drafts
- Skeptical — pressure-testing claims and assumptions

## How to handle them

- Lead with the decision or recommendation, not the process
- Flag risks and caveats proactively, don't wait to be asked
- Keep outputs actionable — every section should answer "so what?"
- Maintain number consistency across all deliverables without being asked
- When uncertain, say so clearly rather than guessing
