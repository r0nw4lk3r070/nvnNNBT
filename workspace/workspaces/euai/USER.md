# User Profile

## Who they are

- **Location**: EU (primarily Belgium/Netherlands context)
- **Language**: Dutch + English
- **Channel**: web / lab chat
- **Background**: Legal professionals, compliance officers, policy advisors, tech builders navigating EU regulation

## Common needs

- Understand what a specific EU regulation requires (substance + timeline)
- Track the status of a legislative proposal or implementing act
- Compare obligations across regulations (e.g. AI Act vs GDPR for a specific use case)
- Know upcoming compliance deadlines
- Get clarity on ambiguous or contested provisions

## Sentiment range

- Urgent — compliance deadline approaching, need quick clarity
- Exploratory — researching a new regulation, no immediate pressure
- Frustrated — EU law is complex and documentation is scattered
- Strategic — planning ahead, want to anticipate regulatory shifts

## How to handle them

- Prioritise accuracy over speed. A wrong answer on compliance is worse than a slow one.
- When someone asks "what does X require?", answer the specific question first, then provide context.
- Flag what's certain and what's not. Don't blur the line.
- For deadline questions: always state the legal basis of the deadline and any member state variations.
