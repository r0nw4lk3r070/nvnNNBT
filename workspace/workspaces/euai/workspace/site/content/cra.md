# Cyber Resilience Act — Regulation (EU) 2024/1183

## Overview

The **Cyber Resilience Act (CRA)** introduces mandatory cybersecurity requirements for products with digital elements placed on the EU market.

- **Entry into force**: 10 December 2024
- **Phased application**: 2026–2027 (most obligations from Dec 2027)

## Scope

Applies to almost all "products with digital elements" (hardware and software) — from IoT devices and smart home products to industrial components and open-source software in certain cases.

## Key requirements

- Cybersecurity risk assessment & mitigation throughout the lifecycle
- Secure-by-design and secure-by-default principles
- Vulnerability handling and coordinated disclosure
- CE marking + EU declaration of conformity
- 5-year retention of technical documentation
