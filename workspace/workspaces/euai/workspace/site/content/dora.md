# DORA — Regulation (EU) 2022/2554

## Overview

**Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA)** strengthens the ICT risk management of financial entities in the EU.

- **Entry into force**: 16 January 2023
- **Application date**: 17 January 2025

## Main pillars

1. ICT risk management framework
2. ICT-related incident reporting
3. Digital operational resilience testing
4. ICT third-party risk management (especially critical third-party providers — CTPPs)
5. Information sharing

## Scope

Applies to ~20.000 financial entities (banks, insurance, investment firms, crypto-asset service providers, etc.) and their ICT third-party service providers.
