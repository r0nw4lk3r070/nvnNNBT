# Data Act — Regulation (EU) 2023/2854

## Overview

The **Data Act** aims to boost the EU data economy by improving access to and use of data generated by connected products and related services.

- **Entry into force**: 11 January 2024
- **Main application**: 12 September 2025

## Key measures

- Right for users to access and share data generated by IoT devices
- B2B and B2G data sharing obligations
- Rules on unfair contractual terms for data access
- Interoperability requirements for data, data spaces and cloud switching
- Safeguards for trade secrets and IP protection

## Relevance for smart home / IoT

Strong implications for manufacturers of connected devices (smart thermostats, cameras, appliances, etc.).
