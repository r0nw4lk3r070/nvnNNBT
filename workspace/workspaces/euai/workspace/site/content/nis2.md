# NIS2 Directive — Directive (EU) 2022/2555

## Overview

The **NIS2 Directive** is the EU's main horizontal cybersecurity legislation. It replaces the original NIS Directive and significantly expands its scope, security requirements, and enforcement.

- **Transposition deadline**: 17 October 2024
- **Application**: 18 October 2024

## Key expansions compared to NIS1

- Much broader scope (18 sectors instead of ~10)
- "Management body" accountability (personal liability possible)
- Supply chain / third-party risk management
- Mandatory incident reporting within 24h (initial) / 72h (final report)
- Stricter supervisory regime + higher fines (up to €10M or 2% global turnover)

## Sectors covered (Annexes I & II)

**Highly critical** (Annex I): energy, transport, banking, financial market infrastructures, health, drinking water, digital infrastructure, public administration, space.
**Important** (Annex II): postal services, waste management, chemicals, food, manufacturing, digital providers, research organisations, etc.
