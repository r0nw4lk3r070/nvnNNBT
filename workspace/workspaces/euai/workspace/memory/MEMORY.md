## Key EU sources & websites

### Core databases
- **EUR-Lex** — https://eur-lex.europa.eu — Official EU law database, consolidated texts, OJ, all 24 languages
- **EP Legislative Observatory (OEIL)** — https://oeil.secure.europarl.europa.eu — Track legislative procedures, votes, rapporteurs, trilogues

### GDPR / Data protection
- **EDPB** — https://www.edpb.europa.eu — Guidelines, binding decisions, consistency mechanism
- **EDPS** — https://www.edps.europa.eu — EU institutions' data protection supervisor, AI oversight

### AI Act
- **EC AI topic page** — https://commission.europa.eu/topics/artificial-intelligence_en
- **AI Office** — https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/ai-office — Centre of AI expertise, implements AI Act
- **AI Act Service Desk + Single Information Platform** — https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en — Compliance support, self-assessment tool
- **AI Act Info (community)** — https://www.aiact-info.eu — News, timeline, unofficial but useful tracker

### NIS2 / Cybersecurity
- **EC NIS2 page** — https://digital-strategy.ec.europa.eu/en/policies/nis2-directive
- **ENISA** — https://www.enisa.europa.eu — EU cybersecurity agency, technical guidance, incident reporting frameworks

### DORA / Financial resilience

### Data Act / Data Governance

## Key deadlines

- AI Act high-risk systems: 2 December 2027
- AI Act full application: 2 August 2027
- NIS2 transposition: 17 October 2024 (passed — member states implementing)
- DORA application: 17 January 2025 (in force)
- Data Act application: 12 September 2025 (in force)
- CRA: adopted 2024, phased application 2027–2029
