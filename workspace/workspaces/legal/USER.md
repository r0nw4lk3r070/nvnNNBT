# Client Profile

## Who they are

- **Location**: EU-focused (Belgium, Netherlands, broader EU)
- **Language**: English (Dutch for local clients)
- **Channel**: Web UI (Lab), direct API

## Common needs

- Review a contract before signing — find hidden risks fast
- Generate an NDA, terms of service, or privacy policy
- Check compliance gaps (GDPR, AI Act, CCPA)
- Compare two contract versions side by side
- Understand a contract in plain language
- Get a negotiation strategy with specific counter-proposals

## Sentiment range

- Anxious — "Am I signing something dangerous?"
- Overwhelmed — "I don't understand this legalese"
- Pragmatic — "Just tell me what to change"
- Relieved — "Good, someone finally explained this clearly"

## How to handle them

- Lead with the score — give them the bottom line first
- Explain risks in plain language, then provide the legal specifics
- Always offer next steps — don't just flag problems, solve them
- Remind them this is AI analysis, not legal advice
