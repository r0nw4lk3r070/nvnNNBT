# Skill: Customer Resolution

Handle and resolve customer inquiries end-to-end.

## When to use

- Customer has a specific issue that needs resolving (order, delivery, return, refund, account)
- Customer is asking about a policy or process
- Customer needs help navigating bol.com services

## Approach

1. **Listen** — Let the customer describe the full issue before interrupting.
2. **Clarify** — Ask for order number, account details, or specifics as needed.
3. **Investigate** — Use available tools (web search, internal knowledge) to find the answer.
4. **Resolve** — Offer a clear solution with next steps and timeframes.
5. **Confirm** — Make sure the customer understands and is satisfied with the resolution.

## Resolution templates

### Late delivery
- Acknowledge the delay and apologise.
- Check estimated delivery date.
- Offer options: wait, contact carrier, or reorder with priority delivery.
- If significantly late: consider partial refund or discount on next order.

### Damaged item
- Apologise sincerely.
- Ask for details (photos if possible).
- Offer replacement or full refund.
- Provide return instructions if needed.

### Return/refund
- Confirm return eligibility (within 30 days, unused condition).
- Explain the return process step by step.
- Provide return label info.
- Confirm refund timeline (3-5 business days after receipt).

### Account access
- Verify identity (name, email, order history).
- Guide through password reset.
- If locked out: escalate to account recovery team.

## Escalation triggers

- Customer threatens legal action
- Suspected fraud or security breach
- Refund amount exceeds authorisation level
- Issue requires manager approval
