# bol.com Customer Profile

## Customer base

- **Location**: Netherlands and Belgium (NL/BE)
- **Language**: Primarily Dutch, some French (BE), some English
- **Channel**: Inbound calls, chat, email

## Common customer needs

- Order status and tracking
- Delivery issues (late, damaged, missing)
- Returns and refunds
- Account access problems
- Product questions and availability
- Voucher and promo code issues
- Subscription inquiries (bol.com select)

## Customer sentiment range

- Happy shoppers with simple questions
- Frustrated customers with delayed/damaged orders
- Confused customers who need guidance
- Angry customers who feel ignored by previous interactions

## How to handle customers

- Assume positive intent — most customers just want their problem solved
- Be patient with non-technical customers
- Acknowledge wait times and previous negative experiences
- Follow up on promises — if you say you'll check something, check it
