# MKT Agents

## Parallel Audit Agents

When running a full marketing audit (`audit` command), 5 parallel agents are launched simultaneously:

### market-content
**Focus:** Content quality, messaging clarity, copy effectiveness
- Headline clarity and specificity (5-second test)
- Value proposition strength
- Body copy persuasion
- Social proof quality
- Content depth and authority
- Brand voice consistency
- **Scores:** Content & Messaging (0-100)

### market-conversion
**Focus:** CRO, funnels, landing pages, signup flows
- CTA effectiveness (clarity, placement, contrast, urgency)
- Form friction (field count, progressive disclosure, inline validation)
- Page layout and visual hierarchy
- Trust signals near conversion points
- Mobile conversion experience
- Signup/checkout flow steps and drop-off risk
- Pricing page effectiveness
- **Scores:** Conversion Optimization (0-100)

### market-competitive
**Focus:** Competitive positioning, market landscape
- Unique positioning clarity
- Competitor awareness signals (comparison pages, "vs" pages)
- Market category definition
- Pricing relative to competitors
- Feature differentiation signals
- Review/reputation presence
- **Scores:** Competitive Positioning (0-100)

### market-technical
**Focus:** Technical SEO, site architecture, page speed
- Title tags, meta descriptions, header hierarchy
- URL structure and internal linking
- Image optimization
- Mobile responsiveness
- Page load speed indicators
- Schema markup / structured data
- Sitemap and robots.txt
- Core Web Vitals signals
- Accessibility basics
- **Scores:** SEO & Discoverability (0-100)

### market-strategy
**Focus:** Overall strategy, pricing, growth opportunities
- Business model clarity
- Pricing strategy
- Growth loops (referral, viral, content, sales-led)
- Retention signals
- Expansion revenue opportunities
- Market timing and trends alignment
- Brand trust signals
- **Scores:** Brand & Trust (0-100), Growth & Strategy (0-100)
