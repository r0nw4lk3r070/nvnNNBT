## Active dossiers

### Digital Omnibus (COM(2025) 837)
- Proposed 19 November 2025 by European Commission
- Status: legislative process — trilogues expected 2026
- Amends: GDPR, AI Act, NIS2, DORA, Data Act, cookie rules
- Key proposed changes:
  - GDPR: legitimate interest for AI development (including sensitive data); breach notification 72→96h; limit data subject rights in abuse cases; research as legitimate interest; personal data definition update per CJEU C-413/23 (relative concept of personal data)
  - AI Act: delay high-risk Annex III → 2 Dec 2027, Annex I → 2 Aug 2028; AI literacy from obligation to encouragement; SME/small mid-cap facilitation; EU sandbox expected 2028
  - Cybersecurity: single incident reporting portal (GDPR+NIS2+DORA); 72→96h reporting
  - Data Act: stronger trade secret safeguards; narrower public-sector access (emergencies only); consolidation with FFNP, DGA, Open Data Directive; cloud-switching relief for SMEs/small mid-caps; lighter framework for custom data processing services under pre-12 Sep 2025 contracts
  - Cookies: fewer banners, one-click consent, browser/OS preference storage; modernisation into GDPR
- Criticism: Amnesty International (Apr 2026) warns of rights rollback, "simplification" as cover for Big Tech interests; EDPB/EDPS expressed concerns (Feb 2026)

### AI Act — enforcement countdown (2 Aug 2026)
- First enforcement signal: Irish DPC Art. 11 documentation request to SaaS company (4 Apr 2026) — candidate-screening tool, 30-day response window, checklist mirrors AI Office internal template
- Commission guidance on Art. 5 prohibited practices (2 Apr 2026) — recommender systems using imperceptible signals (dwell time, micro-engagement, scroll velocity) in gray zone; need documented user-interest test; ad-tech and short-video platforms first affected
- GPAI Code of Practice v2.1 (3 Apr 2026) — training data transparency template (6 pages); domain breakdowns, language coverage, synthetic data yes/no required; 10^25 FLOP threshold; voluntary but presumption of conformity if signed
- AI Office FAQ update (5 Apr 2026) — fine-tuning can make fine-tuner a "provider" if modification "substantially changes" intended purpose or risk profile
- National signals:
  - Spain AESIA: priority audits Q3 2026 (recruitment tools, credit scoring, biometric categorization)
  - Netherlands AP: 1 Apr 2026 notice — emotion recognition in workplace prohibited per Art. 5 from 2 Aug, no exceptions for "wellness" framings; direct shot at HR-tech vendors
  - Germany BSI: revised AI security baseline 3 Apr 2026 — de facto standard for Art. 15 cybersecurity safeguards when selling into Germany

### Cybersecurity — convergence
- EC AWS breach by ShinyHunters (24 Mar 2026, confirmed 30 Mar) — >350GB exfiltrated (databases, contracts, mail server dumps); 90GB archive on dark web; Commission internal systems not affected. Second EC breach in 2026 (February: MDM platform compromise for staff devices)
- First EU cyber sanctions 2026 (16 Mar 2026) — 3 entities + 2 individuals:
  - Integrity Technology Group (China): products used to compromise >65,000 devices across 6 EU member states (2022-2023)
  - Anxun Information Technology (China): hacking services targeting EU critical infrastructure; 2 co-founders individually sanctioned
  - Emennet Pasargad (Iran): breached French subscriber DB, compromised ad billboards during 2024 Paris Olympics (disinformation), compromised Swedish SMS service
- NIS2 amendments proposed 20 Jan 2026 (jurisdiction, scope, certification) as part of CSA2 package
- NIS2 Belgium: implementation in progress, supervisors active
- DORA active enforcement — BaFin and other national regulators conducting audits throughout 2026
- CRA reporting deadline 11 Sep 2026 approaching
