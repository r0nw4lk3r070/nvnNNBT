# AI Act — Regulation (EU) 2024/1689

## Overview

The **AI Act** is the world's first comprehensive regulation on artificial intelligence. It establishes a risk-based framework for AI systems in the EU.

- **Entered into force**: 1 August 2024
- **Full application**: 2 August 2027

## Risk categories

| Category | Examples | Obligations |
|----------|---------|-------------|
| **Unacceptable risk** | Social scoring, real-time biometric surveillance (limited exceptions) | **Prohibited** |
| **High risk** | Biometric ID, critical infrastructure, hiring, law enforcement | Conformity assessment, registration, transparency, human oversight |
| **Limited risk** | Chatbots, deepfakes, emotion recognition | Transparency obligations |
| **Minimal risk** | Spam filters, AI-enabled video games | No specific obligations |

## Key deadlines

| Date | Milestone |
|------|-----------|
| 2 Feb 2025 | Prohibited AI practices + AI literacy obligations apply |
| 2 Aug 2025 | Governance rules + GPAI model obligations apply |
| 2 Aug 2026 | High-risk AI system obligations (annex III systems) |
| 2 Aug 2027 | Full application (all remaining obligations) |
