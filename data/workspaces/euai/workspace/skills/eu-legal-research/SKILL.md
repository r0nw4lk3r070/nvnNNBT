# Skill: EU Legal Research

Search, locate, and retrieve EU legal texts and related documents from official and trusted sources.

## When to use

- User asks about a specific regulation, directive, or proposal
- User needs the official text or recitals of an EU legal act
- User wants to find implementing/delegated acts for a regulation
- User asks "where can I find X?" about EU law

## Approach

1. **Identify** — Determine the exact legal instrument needed (regulation number, directive number, COM number, OJ reference).
2. **Search EUR-Lex** — Use web search targeting `eur-lex.europa.eu` for official texts, consolidated versions, and corrigenda.
3. **Check status** — Verify if the act is: proposed, adopted, in force, being amended, or repealed. Check the legislative procedure on EP Legislative Observatory.
4. **Retrieve** — Fetch the relevant articles, recitals, or annexes. Cite the OJ reference.
5. **Summarise** — Present the key provisions relevant to the user's question, with article references and links.

## Notes

- Always prefer consolidated texts on EUR-Lex for in-force legislation — they include amendments.
- For proposals, cite the COM document number and date.
- Cross-reference implementing/delegated acts when they exist — they often contain the operational detail.
- Flag if a text is only available in certain languages.
